Kenko Miura (born July 30, 1979) is a Japanese racing driver who currently competes as part of the NASCAR Whelen Euro Series. Miura previously competed in the Asian Formula 3 Series. Miura has competed in a total of 22 Euro Elite 1 races since 2017 and in 17 Euro Elite 2 races between 2017 and 2018. He won the 2018 Challenger's Trophy over Dario Caso.
