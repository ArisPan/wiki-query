Kalpana Chawla Government Medical College  (KCGMC) is a co-educational Medical College located in Karnal, Haryana, India.

History
Construction began in late 2013 and is almost complete. It has a fully functional, state of the art opd and hospital.

Accreditation
The Medical College is affiliated to Pandit Bhagwat Dayal Sharma University of Health Sciences, Rohtak, Haryana. Approved by MCI for intake of 120 MBBS students every year.

Hospital
It is associated with Govt. Hospital, Karnal which serves as the teaching hospital.Government of Haryana named the college in honour of the astronaut Kalpana Chawla.
