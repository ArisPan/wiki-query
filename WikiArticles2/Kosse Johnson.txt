David "Kosse" Johnson (July 13, 1932 â€“ September 3, 1996) was an American football player. Johnson played college football at the fullback position for the Rice Owls football team from 1951 to 1953. He was selected by the Associated Press and the Football Writers Association of America as a first-team player on their respective 1953 College Football All-America Teams. He later worked as a high school football coach in Texas.  He was inducted into the Rice Athletic Hall of Fame in 1972.
